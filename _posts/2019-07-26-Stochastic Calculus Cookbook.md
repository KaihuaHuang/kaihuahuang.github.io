# Brownian Motion
## 1. Definition

- Continuous sample path but nondifferentiable
- Independent increment

> $W_{t_1}-W_0$, $W_{t_2} - W_{t_1}$ ... $W_{t_n} - W_{t_{n-1}}$ are i.i.d

- Each of the increments is normally distributed
